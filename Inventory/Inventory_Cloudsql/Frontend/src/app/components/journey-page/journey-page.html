Hey, meet Sarah!

She is an Inventory Manager for a major retail chain. Her day-to-day challenge is managing inventory across 50+ stores in the busy Pacific Northwest region. Her primary goal is simple: highlight the ability to foresee shortage in stock, and order it in advance to avoid it.

Inventory chaos in the modern retail world

Demand is unpredictable, supplier lead times are uncertain, and deciding when and how much to reorder is often manual and error-prone.This leads to massive write-offs from overstock and lost sales from stockouts. Sarah needs a smarter way to plan inventory.

Introducing Sarah’s Game-Changer

The Evaluator Validation Application brings together data, models, and intelligent agents to recommend the right actions at the right time. From predicting shortages to automating reorders, this is Sarah’s new operating system for inventory.
