Emma, Trend-Savvy Shopper

Meet Emma, she loves fashion but hates wasting time scrolling through endless product lists. She wants to find the perfect outfit quickly—whether it’s a chic blazer or something that matches her mood. Her goal? A search experience that understands her style, not just keywords.

Lost in the Scroll: When Search Misses the Mark

Today’s e-commerce search feels broken. Semantic intent is lost, hybrid relevance is missing, and natural language queries like ‘Show me a summer dress under $100’ often fail. The result? Frustration and abandoned carts

Fashion Meets Intelligence: AI-Powered Product Discovery

Enter the AI-Powered Product Finder—built on AlloyDB for PostgreSQL and Cloud SQL. This web-based application transforms product discovery with semantic search, hybrid relevance scoring, and natural language-to-SQL conversion. It’s where fashion meets intelligence.



The Journey of Emma

Now, let’s step into Emma’s world. Her journey is effortless: she types ‘Show me a red cocktail dress under $150’—and AI does the rest. Behind the scenes, AlloyDB retrieves semantically similar products using vector embeddings, applies hybrid scoring with ScaNN and GIN indexes, converts her query into SQL, and even summarizes results. Emma gets exactly what she wants—fast, relevant, and personalized.
