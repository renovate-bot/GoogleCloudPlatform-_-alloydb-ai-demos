Meet Arjun – Data Scientist on a Mission

Meet Arjun, a Data Scientist working on autonomous vehicle safety models. Arjun needs curated video clips of real-world highway incidents—motorbike crashes, lane-change collisions, and near-miss scenarios—to train and fine-tune his models. His challenge? Hours of raw footage make finding relevant clips painfully slow.

When Critical Clips Hide in Endless Footage

Today, video data exploration is a bottleneck. Analysts and safety engineers manually scrub through endless footage, wasting time and risking incomplete datasets. Keyword-based tagging often misses context, making it hard to find specific scenarios like ‘motorbike accidents on highways.’ This slows innovation and increases operational costs

Video Intelligence, Powered by AI

Introducing the AI-Powered Highway Incident Video Finder—a web-based application that transforms video search. Powered by AlloyDB and multimodal vector search, it understands natural language queries and retrieves contextually accurate video segments instantly. No manual browsing, no guesswork—just precise results.
